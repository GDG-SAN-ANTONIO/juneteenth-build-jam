Juneteenth Build Jam · Data Sources

Data & Resources for Your Build

Real data makes a better demo. Pick one source for your track, drop the link or a sample into the chat, and let the AI work with it. Treat every AI output as a draft to verify — especially grants, coordinates, and any official-looking claim.

Foundational (any track)

  • City of San Antonio Open Data — city datasets, open by default — data.sanantonio.gov
  • COSA GIS Open Data — zoning/planning maps, council districts, parks, historic sites, impervious cover, neighborhood association registry — opendata-cosagis.opendata.arcgis.com
  • Texas Open Data Portal — statewide data plus a San Antonio slice — data.texas.gov
  • U.S. Census / American Community Survey — demographics, income, poverty, vehicle access by tract — data.census.gov

🛍️ Stand Up Shop — Economic Empowerment

  • City Economic Development: Programs & Grants — current local business grants/programs — sa.gov/Directory/Departments/EDD/Programs-Grants
  • Grants.gov — federal grant opportunities — grants.gov
  • U.S. Small Business Administration — programs and local SBDC counseling — sba.gov
  • LiftFund — San Antonio-based CDFI microlender — liftfund.com

📜 Griot — Preserving History

  • The Portal to Texas History (UNT) — SA newspapers, HemisFair '68, Folklife Festival images — texashistory.unt.edu
  • UTSA Digital Collections — photographs, oral histories, San Antonio & Chicano history — digital.utsa.edu
  • San Antonio Public Library — Texana & Genealogy — local history special collections — mysapl.org
  • Digital Public Library of America — national digital archives — dp.la
  • Library of Congress — national primary sources — loc.gov

🩺 PlainCare — Community Health

  • USDA Food Access Research Atlas — census-tract food-desert mapping — ers.usda.gov/data-products/food-access-research-atlas
  • CDC PLACES — local health estimates down to census-tract level — cdc.gov/places
  • Texas Health Data (DSHS) — state health datasets — healthdata.dshs.texas.gov
  • HRSA Find a Health Center — federally funded clinics by location — findahealthcenter.hrsa.gov
  • 2-1-1 Texas — social-service and resource directory — 211texas.org
  • VIA Metropolitan Transit — routes and paratransit for transit-gap questions — viainfo.net

🗣️ Speak Up — Civic Engagement

  • City Clerk: Council Agendas & Minutes — official meeting agendas and minutes — sanantonio.gov/Clerk/Legislative/City-Council-Agendas-Minutes
  • SASpeakUp — agendas for council, committees, and boards/commissions — saspeakup.com
  • Legistar — searchable legislation/agenda system — sanantonio.legistar.com
  • Texas Legislature Online — state bills and statutes — capitol.texas.gov
  • Bexar County — county government and records — bexar.org

🌳 Block Scan — Environmental Justice

⚠️ Heads up

EPA removed EJScreen from public access on February 5, 2025, and the related CEJST tool was also removed. The links below point to volunteer-maintained reconstructions — useful, but treat the data as unofficial and verify before relying on it.

  • EJScreen (volunteer reconstruction) — environmental burden + demographic mapping — screening-tools.com/epa-ejscreen
  • AirNow — current air quality by location — airnow.gov
  • EPA ECHO — facility enforcement & compliance history — echo.epa.gov
  • TCEQ — Texas environmental data and complaints — tceq.texas.gov
  • San Antonio 311 service requests — illegal dumping and code complaints — data.sanantonio.gov
