"Neighborhood vision + Gemini → an instant co-op storefront and grant match."

What you'll build (45 min)

Type in a community initiative (like a garden). AI instantly generates a branded co-op profile, a competitive pricing strategy, an SEO-friendly description, and matches the project to a local civic grant.

The moment it clicks

"Wait, it found a $5,000 micro-grant we actually qualify for?"

Things to think about

  • Keep it text-based today. No complex payment integrations or databases.
  • Your signature detail. What makes your storefront unique? Gen-Z aesthetics, or formal municipal proposals?
  • Test with wild ideas. Try a "laser tag arena" first to see edge cases.

"Dense city council transcripts + Gemini → an instant Civic Insight Report and mobilization alert."

What you'll build (45 min)

Paste in a wall of text from a dry zoning meeting transcript. The AI identifies the major decisions, highlights the displacement risk, and generates a clear "Community Action" alert with deadlines.

The moment it clicks

"It read a 10-page zoning PDF and instantly flagged the exact block facing a tax spike."

Things to think about

  • Focus on extraction. Pull the impact, not just a summary. Tell the AI exactly what "vulnerable community impact" means.
  • Your signature detail. Does it output as an email template, a WhatsApp forward, or a formal memo?

"A photo or text of a neighborhood hazard + Gemini → a drafted, formal 311 service request or petition."

What you'll build (45 min)

Describe an environmental hazard (like a chemical smell or illegal dumping). The AI classifies the violation and instantly drafts a formal, structured petition ready to be sent to Code Enforcement or the EPA.

The moment it clicks

"It turned my angry rant about the smell into a perfectly formatted, legally-sound EPA petition."

Things to think about

  • Keep the output format strict. The magic is in making the output look like a real ticket. Use headers and a formal tone.
  • Your signature detail. Does it generate a single email, a mass petition, or a social media blast?
