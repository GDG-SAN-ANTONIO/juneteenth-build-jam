# Speak Up

**Pitch:** Dense city council transcripts + Gemini → an instant Civic Insight Report and mobilization alert.

## What you'll build (45 minutes)
Paste in a wall of text from a dry zoning meeting transcript or neighborhood association notes. The AI instantly identifies the major decisions, highlights the displacement risk to vulnerable residents, and generates a clear "Community Action" alert with exact deadlines for public comment.

## The moment it clicks
*"It read a 10-page zoning PDF and instantly flagged the exact block facing a tax spike."*

## Things to think about
- **Focus on extraction.** Your goal is to get the AI to pull the *impact*, not just summarize the meeting. Tell the AI exactly what "vulnerable community impact" means in your prompt.
- **Your signature detail.** How urgent does the alert look? Does it output as an email template, a WhatsApp forward, or a formal memo?
- **Test with a real transcript.** Find a public PDF from a recent city council meeting and see if the AI catches the zoning changes.

## Where to take it after the jam (Polished Version)
- PDF file upload for direct transcript processing
- Automated email drafting to local representatives
- Calendar integration to save public comment deadlines
- Social media share buttons for quick mobilization
- Fact-checking layer comparing proposals to historical zoning data

## Tech you'll touch
- Antigravity (AI-driven IDE)
- Python + google-genai SDK (backend)
- Google Gemini API (Large context window summarization & impact analysis)
- HTML / CSS / JS (frontend)
